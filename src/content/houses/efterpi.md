Full website description

House Efterpi is a restored traditional Cretan stone house in the hillside village of Mavrikiano, just above Elounda. Positioned on the ground floor and organized around a private enclosed courtyard, it offers a different kind of stay from the sea-view or pool-focused homes in your collection. Here, the atmosphere is more inward and sheltered: stone walls, shaded outdoor space, and handcrafted details create a sense of calm that feels rooted in the older rhythm of the village.

The defining feature of Efterpi is its courtyard living. The private outdoor space includes a summer kitchen, BBQ, and marble dining table, making it especially well suited to slow breakfasts, evening meals, and long hours spent outside. Inside, the house combines traditional materials with a warm and practical layout for up to 5 guests, including open-plan sleeping space on the main level and a loft bedroom above.
