Full website description

House Erato is a restored traditional Cretan stone house in the hillside village of Mavrikiano, just above Elounda. It offers a more private and self-contained stay than the shared-pool homes in your collection, combining authentic architecture with the comfort of a private swimming pool and shaded outdoor living. The house provides 60 m² of interior space for up to 4 guests, making it well suited to couples, a small family, or travelers who prefer privacy over a more communal setup.
Source: the details you provided in your Erato notes.

The outdoor space defines the experience. Erato opens onto a private pool terrace with room for sun loungers, outdoor seating, and slow meals outside. The setting looks toward the olive groves, surrounding mountains, and the village, giving the house a more inward and tranquil atmosphere than the sea-view houses in the collection. A shaded dining area, BBQ, and balcony make it easy to spend most of the day outdoors, whether for breakfast, reading, or a quiet evening after returning from the coast.
Source: the details you provided in your Erato notes.

Inside, the house balances traditional stone character with practical comfort. The layout includes a living area, kitchen, dining area, one enclosed bedroom, and additional sleeping space, making it flexible for different kinds of guests while retaining the feel of a genuine village home rather than a standard holiday unit. Erato also works well as a paired stay: when booked together with House Clio above, the two homes can accommodate up to 8 guests, with the pool becoming a shared amenity for that combined booking. From the house, guests can walk to Elounda in around 10 minutes or use it as a base for exploring Plaka, Spinalonga, Agios Nikolaos, and the wider Lasithi area.
