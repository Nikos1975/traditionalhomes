Full website description

House Margarita is a traditional multi-level Cretan stone house in the hillside village of Mavrikiano, just above Elounda. It is one of the larger homes in your collection, offering 120 m² of interior space and a layout that feels rooted in the architecture of the village rather than separated from it. The house is best understood through its outdoor living: a generous 35 m² veranda with wide views of the sea and mountains, designed for long breakfasts, quiet afternoons, and evening meals outdoors.

Inside, Margarita combines natural stone, handcrafted details, and open-plan sleeping areas with the practical comforts needed for a relaxed stay. It accommodates up to 6 guests, making it suitable for families, friends, or travelers who want a more spacious base in Elounda without losing the character of a traditional home. An outdoor kitchen and BBQ extend the living space beyond the walls of the house, while the elevated village position creates a sense of privacy and calm above the busier waterfront below.

From the house, guests can reach Elounda beach, the harbor, cafés, shops, and tavernas in around 10 minutes on foot. Free private parking is available about 70 meters away, with additional public parking nearby. Margarita also works well as part of a small group stay, since Leonidas is about 5 meters away and Argyro about 20 meters away, allowing guests to stay close while retaining separate accommodation.

Practical note for the page

House Margarita has a multi-level layout with stairs, split levels, and an elevated veranda, so it may be less suitable for very young children, elderly guests, or anyone looking for step-free accommodation.
