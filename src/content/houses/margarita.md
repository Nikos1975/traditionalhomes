### A Larger Village House
House Margarita is one of the roomier houses in the Mavrikiano collection. It accommodates up to six guests and suits families or friends who want a sea-view veranda and more separation across levels. The house has two bedrooms, one bathroom, and internal stairs.

### The Interior Layout
The 120 m2 layout follows the slope of the village, with living and sleeping areas spread across more than one level. Stone, timber, and a fully equipped kitchen keep the house practical for longer stays. The vertical arrangement gives guests some separation, but it also means stairs are part of daily use.

### Sea-View Veranda
The veranda is the main gathering space, with views toward Elounda Bay and the surrounding hills. An outdoor kitchen and barbecue make it useful for meals at home.

### Access And Setting
Free private parking is about 70 m away. The house is not step-free, and guests should expect at least one step from the path to the entrance plus internal stairs. The bathroom is accessed via the courtyard area, which is important to know before booking.
