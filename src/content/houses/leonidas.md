Full website description

House Leonidas is a small traditional stone house in the hillside village of Mavrikiano, just above Elounda. Designed for couples, solo travelers, and small families, it offers a quieter way to experience Crete — close to the coast, but slightly above it, where the views open and the pace slows.

Inside, the house combines traditional Cretan character with a simple, comfortable layout. The most distinctive feature is the sea-view bedroom, where the day begins with light over the bay rather than the noise of the town below. A private balcony extends that feeling outdoors, creating a natural place for morning coffee, evening wine, or a slow moment between excursions. The house accommodates up to 3 guests within a 50 m² footprint, making it best suited to travelers who appreciate intimacy, authenticity, and a compact village setting.

Leonidas is also well placed for exploring the area. From the house, guests can walk to Elounda beach, tavernas, cafés, shops, and the harbor in about 10 minutes, while Plaka and boats to Spinalonga are a short drive away. Free parking is available nearby, and the house forms part of your wider collection of traditional homes in Mavrikiano, with Margarita and Argyro only a few meters away for guests traveling together but preferring separate accommodation.

Practical note for the page

House Leonidas includes an internal staircase and a raised sea-view balcony, so it may be less suitable for guests with reduced mobility, very young children, or anyone looking for step-free accommodation.
