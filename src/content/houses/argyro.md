### A Sea-View House In Mavrikiano
House Argyro is a traditional stone house in the hillside village of Mavrikiano. It suits up to four guests who want two bedrooms, a sea-view veranda, and a quieter setting above Elounda. The house is arranged over two floors, with internal stairs between levels.

### The Interior Layout
The 85 m2 layout includes two bedrooms, one bathroom, living space, and a fully equipped kitchen. Stone walls and timber details keep the character of the building visible, while the plan gives small families or two couples separate sleeping space.

### Veranda And Courtyard
The first-floor veranda is the main outdoor space, with views toward Elounda Bay. A private courtyard with an open kitchenette and barbecue gives guests another place to prepare food or sit outside.

### Access And Setting
Free private parking is about 80 m away. The house is not step-free, and guests should expect at least one step from the path to the entrance as well as internal stairs inside the house.
