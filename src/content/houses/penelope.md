Full website description

House Penelope is a traditional stone home located on the first floor above House Demetra in the hillside village of Mavrikiano, just above Elounda. It combines the atmosphere of a restored village house with the practical advantages of outdoor living and access to a shared swimming pool. The house offers 70 m² of interior space and accommodates up to 6 guests, making it suitable for families, couples traveling together, or small groups who want a peaceful base close to the sea but removed from the busier waterfront.

Practical note for the page

House Penelope is accessed by traditional exterior stone stairs and also includes an internal staircase to the mezzanine sleeping area. Because of the stairs, upper-floor access, and shared pool, it may be less suitable for guests with reduced mobility, very young children, or anyone looking for step-free accommodation.
