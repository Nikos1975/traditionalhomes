Full website description

House Demetra is a traditional stone-built home on the ground floor in the hillside village of Mavrikiano, just above Elounda. It is designed for guests who want a more settled and practical base, with easy outdoor access and the atmosphere of a restored village house rather than a standard holiday apartment. The home accommodates up to 4 guests across two bedrooms, making it well suited to couples, a small family, or friends traveling together.

The defining feature of Demetra is its relationship to the outdoor space. The house opens toward a shared swimming pool, used together with House Penelope above, and also includes a private veranda / courtyard-style seating area for meals, reading, or quiet evenings outside. Compared with the sea-view houses in your collection, Demetra is shaped more by its sense of enclosure and calm: stone walls, shaded outdoor space, and direct access to the pool create a slower, more grounded rhythm of stay. The surrounding outlook is toward the olive groves and mountains, reinforcing that quieter village character.

From Demetra, guests can reach Elounda beach, tavernas, the harbor, and local shops in about 10 minutes on foot, while Plaka, Spinalonga, and Agios Nikolaos are easy to reach by car.
