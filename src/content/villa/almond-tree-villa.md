### A Hillside Villa In Vrouchas
Almond Tree Villa sits above the coast in Vrouchas, separate from the Mavrikiano village houses. It is suited to larger families or groups who want one private base rather than separate houses. The setting looks toward Elounda Bay, Spinalonga, the Mirabello Gulf, the garden, and the pool.

### The Interior Layout
The 230 m2 villa accommodates up to 10 guests across five bedrooms and two bathrooms. The split-level layout gives the group room to spread out, with shared living areas, a well-fitted kitchen, and upper-floor bedrooms reached by internal stairs. Climate control, laundry facilities, Wi-Fi, a smart TV, and an espresso machine support longer stays.

### Pool, Garden, And Outdoor Dining
The private pool measures 9 m x 4 m and sits beside the main terrace with sun loungers. Outdoor dining areas, a summer kitchen, and a barbecue make the villa practical for meals at home. The garden and pool terrace are the main outdoor spaces, with open views across the bay and surrounding hills.

### Access And Setting
The villa is in a hillside village location. Street parking is about 30 m away, with several steps from parking to the entrance. The approach road is steep but suitable for standard passenger cars. The villa is around a 10-minute drive from Mavrikiano, with Plaka and the coast nearby by car.
