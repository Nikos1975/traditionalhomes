## Recommended Day-Trip Itinerary: The Mirabello Circuit

This sequence keeps travel simple and places more exposed stops earlier in the day.

* **08:30 AM | Departure to Plaka:** Leave Mavrikiano for Plaka.
* **09:00 AM | Spinalonga Visit:** Take one of the early boats from Plaka and allow around 1.5 to 2 hours on site.
* **11:30 AM | Lunch in Plaka:** Return to Plaka for lunch by the waterfront.
* **01:30 PM | Swim at Plaka Beach:** Use the nearby pebble beach for a short swim break.
* **03:00 PM | Sunken City of Olous:** Continue south to the isthmus area for a walk or snorkel stop.
* **04:30 PM | Kolokitha Beach:** Continue to Kolokitha by dirt road or on foot over the hill route.
* **07:00 PM | Return to Mavrikiano:** Return to Mavrikiano in the evening.

## Essential Packing List

For this route mix of boat transfer, walking, and beach stops, these items are practical:

* **Footwear for varied terrain:** Sturdy walking sandals or sneakers (essential for the uneven stones of Spinalonga and the hike to Kolokitha).
* **Water shoes:** Highly recommended for getting in and out of the water comfortably at Plaka's pebble beach and around the rocky ruins of Olous.
* **Snorkeling gear:** Useful for observing submerged stones around the Sunken City of Olous.
* **Sun protection:** Bring a wide-brimmed hat, sunglasses, and reef-safe sunscreen.
* **Hydration & Snacks:** Bring plenty of water, especially for the hike over the Kolokitha peninsula where there are no facilities.
* **Light layers:** A lightweight, breathable long-sleeve shirt or cover-up for boat rides and intense midday sun.
* **Dry bag:** To protect your phone, wallet, and keys from sea spray on the boat to Spinalonga or while relaxing on the beaches.

## Spinalonga Visitor Information (2026 Season)

These figures are practical planning notes and may change by operator or season.

### Operating Hours (Summer Season: April 1 - October 31)
The archaeological site is open daily from **08:30 AM to 6:00 PM** (18:00).
* The last admission is typically 30 minutes before closing.
* *Note: The site is closed to the general public during the winter months (November 1st - March 31st).*

### Site Entrance Tickets
You will purchase your entrance tickets at the ticket office immediately upon disembarking on the island.
* **Full Price:** €20
* **Reduced Price:** €10 (Seniors over 65)
* **Free Admission:** Children, young people, and students under 25 (EU citizens).

### Boat Transfers
Boats operate continuously throughout the day starting around 8:30 AM or 9:00 AM, aligning with the site's opening hours. There is no need to book these small transfers in advance; you can simply arrive at the docks.

#### From Plaka
This is the shortest and most convenient crossing.
* **Ticket Price:** €10 to €12 for a round trip.
* **Travel Time:** About 7 to 10 minutes.
* **Frequency:** Every 30 minutes.

#### From Schisma Eloundas
A slightly longer, scenic route across the bay.
* **Ticket Price:** €12 to €14 for a round trip.
* **Travel Time:** About 20 to 25 minutes.
* **Frequency:** Every 30 minutes.

> **Planning note:** Taking an early boat from Plaka (around 8:30 AM to 9:00 AM) can mean cooler conditions and shorter queues.
